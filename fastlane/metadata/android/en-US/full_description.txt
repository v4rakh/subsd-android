subsd is a music player daemon for Subsonic-compatible servers such as Navidrome.

This Android app acts as a satellite: it connects to a running subsd daemon via gRPC and plays audio locally on the device. The subsd web UI or remote CLI acts as the remote control.

Features:
- Browse artists, albums, and tracks from your Subsonic library
- Search your entire music collection
- Full queue management
- Playlist support
- Satellite management — switch between registered playback devices
- Runs as a background service for uninterrupted playback

Requires a self-hosted subsd daemon (https://github.com/varakh/subsd).
